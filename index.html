Privacy Policy — RocketPlay Days Free

Last updated: July 1, 2026

RocketPlay Days Free ("the App") is an unofficial fan-made habit tracker. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by RocketPlay Casino. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

The App stores the following data:

  • Locally on your device: your daily check-in status (free/played), streak history, and calendar data. This data never leaves your device unless you register for the leaderboard.
  • If you register for the Leaderboard (optional): a nickname and email address, plus your current and longest streak, stored in our database (Firebase Firestore) to display public rankings.
  • Push notification token: if you allow notifications, a device token is generated by Firebase Cloud Messaging to deliver notifications to your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your personal streak and progress within the App.
  • To show your nickname and streak on the public Leaderboard, if you register.
  • To send you push notifications about the app or related offers, if you grant permission.
  • To contact you by email about the challenge/leaderboard, if you register.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is stored using Google Firebase (Firestore, Cloud Messaging) and is subject to Google's data processing terms.

4. Your Choices

  • Leaderboard registration is fully optional. You can use the App and track your progress without registering.
  • You can disable push notifications at any time in your device settings.
  • To request deletion of your registered data (email, nickname, streak record), contact us at the email below.

5. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children under 18 and we do not knowingly collect data from minors.
